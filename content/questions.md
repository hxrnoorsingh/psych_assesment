# Assessment Questions

## Axis PA: Personality Patterns
1. I often use the internet to avoid dealing with my real-life problems.
2. I feel restless or irritable when I cannot use the internet.
3. I have tried to cut down my internet use but failed.
4. I often stay online longer than I intended.
5. My internet use has negatively affected my relationships with family or friends.
6. I lie to others about how much time I spend online.
7. I feel a strong urge to get back online when I am offline.

## Axis MA: Mental Functioning Capacities
1. I find it difficult to focus on my schoolwork because I am thinking about the internet.
2. I often act on impulse without thinking about the consequences.
3. I struggle to reflect on my own emotions and behaviors.
4. I have trouble understanding why I feel or act the way I do.
5. I find it hard to resist the temptation to check my phone or computer.
6. I often feel mentally exhausted from spending too much time online.
7. I have difficulty planning my daily activities effectively.

## Axis SA: Symptomatic Distress
1. I often feel anxious or nervous when I am offline.
2. I have felt down, depressed, or hopeless recently.
3. I get easily annoyed or angry when interrupted while online.
4. I have trouble sleeping because of my internet use.
5. I feel lonely or isolated even when I am connected with others online.
6. I feel overwhelmed by my emotions and don't know how to cope.
