# Assessment Questions

## Axis P: Personality & Relational Patterns
1. I often feel that my relationships are unstable or chaotic.
2. I find it hard to trust others completely.
3. I tend to rely on others to make decisions for me.
4. I often feel empty or bored when I am alone.
5. I struggle to understand what others are feeling.
6. I often feel that others are out to get me or take advantage of me.
7. I have a hard time controlling my anger in relationships.
8. I often feel misunderstood by those close to me.
9. I tend to avoid close relationships to protect myself.
10. I often feel that I am not good enough for others.
11. I find it difficult to express my true feelings to others.

## Axis M: Mental Functioning Capacities
1. I am able to reflect on my own thoughts and feelings.
2. I can usually calm myself down when I get upset.
3. I have a clear sense of who I am and what I value.
4. I am able to see things from other people's perspectives.
5. I can maintain a sense of self-worth even when things go wrong.
6. I am able to make realistic plans and stick to them.
7. I can distinguish between my internal feelings and external reality.
8. I am able to express my emotions in words rather than acting them out.
9. I feel capable of learning from my mistakes.
10. I can tolerate uncertainty without becoming overly anxious.
11. I am able to maintain meaningful relationships over time.

## Axis S: Symptom Distress
1. I have been feeling down, depressed, or hopeless.
2. I have felt nervous, anxious, or on edge.
3. I have had trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much.
4. I have felt tired or had little energy.
5. I have had a poor appetite or overate.
6. I have felt bad about myself — or that I am a failure or have let myself or my family down.
7. I have had trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television.
8. I have been moving or speaking so slowly that other people could have noticed. Or the opposite — being so fidgety or restless that I have been moving around a lot more than usual.
9. I have had thoughts that I would be better off dead, or of hurting myself.
10. I have felt overwhelmed by my emotions.
11. I have felt disconnected from reality or my surroundings.
